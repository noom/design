App Interface Guidelines

We want to create harmonious experiences for our users at Noom, and we do this by applying solutions consistently across our product suite, and promoting learnability. This section of the These guidelines are intended to help standardize mockups and design for Noom's core application: Noom Coach. This does not include each individual element or interaction in the app, but rather aims to provide the design patterns which make up the core Noom Coach Features. This guideline will be an on-going project, and will be continuously revised and expanded to suit changing product needs.

General

The Noom design system is comprised of a series of page design elements and modules which work together to shape the Noom experience. Use the rules outlined in this design system to create new features or functionality to ensure a consistent user experience.
